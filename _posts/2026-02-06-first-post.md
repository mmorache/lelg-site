Welcome to the website for my pop-punk project, **Loose Ends, Long Goodbyes**.

Social media peaked for me with Tom on my Top 8, so this humble DIY website is my first step toward introducing my music. The songs are written, performed, and recorded by me, Mathias, occasionally with help from friends. It’s both an homage to the pop-punk I grew up on and a continuation of it. Hopefully a little wiser for the years in between.

The debut album is a ways off, but I want to start sharing demos, updates, and thoughts. More soon.
